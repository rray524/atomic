Office Design, Refurbishment and Fitout

Office Design, Refurbishment and Fitout Sydney Office Fit-Outs is a privately owned Australian building services company based in Sydney and has completed and ongoing projects across New South Wales. Sydney Office Fit-Outs is built and operated with the best service principles and commitment to on-time and within budget completion.

We are a growing operation and have successfully built a reputation within Sydney’s competitive marketplace in the residential and commercial refurbishment sectors and offer a comprehensive range of building trades, including plasterboard ceilings, partitions, suspended ceiling systems, glazing, aluminium framing (office partitions), painting and cleaning with a personalized service tailored to each individual customer.

Across our broad portfolio of projects and customers, our principles and commitment remain the same. Sydney Office Fit-Outs takes a high collaborative approach with our clientele and their appointed consultants and whoever is authorised by the client to work on the project. Our highly skilled and professional staff work together as a team to get the best results to complete the project to meet the client’s expectations.
